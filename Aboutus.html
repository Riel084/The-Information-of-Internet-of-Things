About Us

We are the students from Gateway's Institute of Science and Technologies

Our goal is to share knowledge about the Internet of Things. Because Many people use IoT in our daily lives without realizing it, and we want to explain how it works and why it matters. By sharing information about IoT, we aim to show its benefits in making tasks easier and improving productivity. Whether in homes or schools. IoT is changing the way we live and work.
