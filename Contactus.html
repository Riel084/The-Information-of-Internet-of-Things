About Us

At Gateway's Institute of Science and Technologies,
we are committed to empowering the next generation of tech enthusiasts by providing quality education in Information and Communication Technology (ICT).
Our initiative focuses on delivering comprehensive knowledge about the advantages of the Internet of Things (IoT) to Grade 12 ICT students, helping them understand its impact on modern technology and future career opportunities. Through interactive learning sessions, real-world applications, and hands-on experiences, we strive to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation. Our goal is to equip students with the skills and insights needed to navigate the evolving digital landscape, ensuring they stay ahead in the fast-growing world of IoT.
